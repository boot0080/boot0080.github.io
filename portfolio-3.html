Fernie Alpine Resort

Logo Design & Branding

Fernie Alpine Resort sits above the legendary town of Fernie – a historical mining community with an unforgettable and eclectic personality. Fernie is known particularly for its high annual snowfall, reportedly the highest of any resort in the Canadian Rockies, and for its powder skiing.
As an avid snowboarder, I created my first personal project as a rebranding of a Canadian resort. When rebranding, I considered application as the most important aspect. The logo would be used frequently and all over the resort. With its badge like design it applies well on ski jackets and apparel along with print and also on chairlifts, etc. I have future plans to push this design a website and brochures for the resort to complete the brand and identity.
