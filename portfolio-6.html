MeasureIt App Design

This project was designed to create an app based on an experience that we frequent. This app was meant to only have a simple function in order to make an experience more enjoyable.MeasureIt is a iOS app allows you to measure the world around you while utilizing the GPS on your iPhone. Simply hit the button to start and again when you are finished and you have your measurement. Need measurements for a home improvement?

Simply log all your data and have it ready for your trip to IKEA. You can even take your measurements and draw it on a photo of your room and your information is never confused. Everyone knows that it’s never fun buying a new bed only to find it can’t get in the front door. App comes fully stocked with different units and a calibration to ensure the best accuracy.
