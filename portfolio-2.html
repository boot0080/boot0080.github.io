Izze Campaign

IZZE is the brand name of a line of carbonated juice drinks produced in Boulder, Colorado. The drinks consist of 70% fruit juice from concentrate, and 30% seltzer water. Izze is an all-natural, no-preservatives-added fruit soda.

CHALLENGE: To create a campaign for the soft drink that best represents the company and the type of product they make while showcasing every type of drink they offer.
