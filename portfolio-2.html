Argo Tea Package Design

This was my first package design. The idea was to create something unique while catering to tea consumers. When walking down the tea isle, many of the tea packages are in a standard cube. The goal was to create a design that stood out against the competitors.

Argo tea is an existing tea company based in Chicago. The package was designed for a loose leaf tea. The look was to remain natural and relaxing which are two main characteristics of tea and tea drinkers.
