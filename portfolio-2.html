Argo Tea

Content · Packaging · Photography

Argo Tea is a popular tea company based in the US. Originally when they began, they hoped to create a company that compared to Starbucks and eventually they grew into a well recognized brand. As a company, they are passionate about being sustainable and delivering quality tea.
With their main product being loose leaf tea, I drew inspiration from tea leaves and the natural taste and feel of tea. The shape of the package is a leaf, with texture applied to complete the look. The idea was to change colours of the package between the different flavours. The shape is unique and functional and stands out against other competitors who use a standard box shape.
