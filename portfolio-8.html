VIA Rail

UX • UI

VIA Rail is the only intercity rail service in Canada. Used frequently by students and business professionals, VIA Rail operates 497 trains per week in eight Canadian provinces. VIA Rail carries approximately 4.1 million passengers annually, the majority on routes along Ontario and Quebec.
As a popular method of travel for many Canadians, VIA Rail currently does not have a mobile application. This app is designed to making tasks easier for their passengers with quick convenience. With passbook compatibility and the ability to book a trip, this app makes their trip simple and convenient. This project is still in progress.
