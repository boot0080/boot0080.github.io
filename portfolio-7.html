Kettleman's Bagel Co.

Logo Design & Branding

Kettleman's Bagel Co. has been Ottawa's maker of traditional Montreal style bagels for 21 years. By rolling, kettling, and baking their bagels in a wood burning-oven using hand picked hardwood, they maintain an unwavering commitment to quality. They capture the traditional and original attention to detail which one expects when they go to an old-fashioned bagel shop.
When rebranding their logo for a school project, I aimed to capture their essence. The goal was to create a logo that was modern and easy to reproduce on multiple different platforms. The flame is used to signify their wood-burning method which is an important part of their brand and identity.
