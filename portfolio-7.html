Portrait - Pencil Drawing

As part of a personal project, I decided to rebrand Fernie Alpine Resport in British Columbia. In my spare time I enjoy snowboarding so I felt it would tie in with my interests. Many mountains have outdated logos that need to be modernized. I wanted to rebrand a large mountain area, but still remain in Canada.

It was challenging because of all the many different applications for this logo. At a ski resort it can be seen on jackets, ski lifts, ski passes, promotional items and all over the village. I hope to later transfer this design to a website and then a mobile app.
