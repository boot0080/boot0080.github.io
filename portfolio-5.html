Tactile Stop Motion - Starbucks

Starbucks Coffee Company

Motion · Advertising

Using stop motion, I created an advertisement for Starbucks Coffee Company. As a coffee lover, I drew inspiration from coffee grinds in my coffee and turned it into something more. Using their iconic logo, I was able to use the simplicity of their brand to create a dynamic piece while still promoting their products. I also used typography to complete the look and ensure that I stayed with the Starbucks look and feel that is so well known worldwide.
