Coffee Addict • Typographer • Illustrator • Adventure Seeker • Devoted Snowboarder

I am a design enthusiast from Ottawa with an education in Graphic Design from Algonquin College. I seek a life filled with adventure and enjoy endless afternoons in coffee shops. I believe that travel is the one thing in life you can buy that makes you richer. My extrovert personality craves meeting new people and sharing ideas and inspiration.

As a designer I am immensely passionate about creating innovative designs and turning concepts into something the whole world can see. I embrace the process and realize sometimes the best work is what you find at 3am. It’s a beautiful thing when passion and a career come together because when you love what you do, you don’t work a day in your life.
