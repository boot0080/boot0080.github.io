Snowboard Magazine

Concept · Lettering & Layout

As another personal project, I wanted to use my love of typography and passion for snowboarding to create a typography layout that could be used in a snowboard magazine. The article is a feature of Canadian Olympic bronze medalist, Mark McMorris. The typography on the opening page features all descriptive words on the athlete and keywords that contribute to his popularity. I often used Snowboard Canada Magazine for inspiration to ensure my design would be appropriate for their magazine.
