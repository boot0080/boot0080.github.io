Mark McMorris Layout

This piece was a small feature on Mark McMorris, a Canadian snowboarder and Olympian. I've always been passionate about handmade type and its appeal and aesthetic. I wanted this to be a fun layout that could be used in a snowboard magazine.

This was another personal project where I tied my love of typography and my love of snowboarding together. I am also an avid reader of snowboard magazines like Snowboard Canada and Transworld Snowboarding. These are the magazines I spent so much time reading and admiring.
