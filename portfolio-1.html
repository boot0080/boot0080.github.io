Izze

Campaign · Advertising · Handmade Typography

Izze is an all-natural, no-preservatives-added fruit soda based in Colorado. Their drinks consist of 70% fruit juice and 30% seltzer water. They focus strongly on not only tasting good but doing good and as a healthy alternatives to other soft drinks.
The goal of this project was to create a campaign for their drinks that adheres to their target audience. The company has three different types of drinks. Izze, Izze Esque and IzzeFortified. The idea for the campaign was to layout all of their important drink features for the consumer in a fun way which reflects their brand and look as a company.
